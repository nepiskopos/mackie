# Welcome to Mackie 👋

**Mackie** is an AI social media assistant for nonprofit organizations.

### What I can do
- **Research your org** — scrape your website and search the web before writing anything
- **Suggest posts** — grounded in research, with source attribution
- **Write platform-specific content** — LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook each get their own tone, length, and hashtag style
- **Remember your voice** — corrections stick across the conversation and across sessions
- **Track your work** — every suggestion and draft goes into a ledger you can query at any time

### Getting started
Tell me your organization's name and website URL. I'll take it from there.
